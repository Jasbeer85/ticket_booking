Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my tickets?

After completing your booking, you will receive an email with your e-tickets attached. You can also download your tickets from the "My Bookings" section of your account.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

Cancellation and change policies vary by event. Generally, you can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. To make changes, please contact our customer support team.

Are there any additional fees?

Yes, a service fee is applied to all bookings. This fee covers the cost of processing your payment and delivering your tickets. The fee amount is displayed during the checkout process before you complete your payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely.
